Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our payment services and processes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, digital wallets, letters of credit, direct debits, and select cryptocurrencies. Our goal is to provide flexible payment options to accommodate the diverse needs of our clients.

How long does it take for payments to process?

Processing times vary by payment method. Credit card payments and digital wallet transactions typically process instantly. Bank transfers usually clear within 1-3 business days, while international transfers may take 3-5 business days. Letters of credit processing depends on the specifics of the transaction and documentation.

Are there any transaction fees?

Transaction fees vary depending on the payment method and transaction volume. We maintain transparent fee structures and offer competitive rates. Volume discounts are available for businesses with high transaction volumes. Please contact our finance department for specific fee information relevant to your business needs.

How secure are your payment systems?

Security is our top priority. We implement robust security measures including end-to-end encryption, PCI DSS compliance, two-factor authentication, and advanced fraud detection systems. Our payment infrastructure undergoes regular security audits and penetration testing to ensure the highest level of security.

Can I set up recurring payments?

Yes, our payment portal allows you to set up recurring payments for regular expenses such as rent, service fees, and utility payments. You can easily manage payment schedules, amounts, and notification preferences through your account dashboard.

What currencies do you support?

We support multiple currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, AED, and many others. Our system automatically handles currency conversion at competitive exchange rates. For specific currency inquiries, please contact our finance department.
